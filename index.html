What We Measure

  1. Lower-body strength — Chair sit-to-stand test (30 seconds)
  2. Muscular endurance — Wall sit hold (timed)
  3. Balance & proprioception — Single-leg balance (eyes open/closed)
  4. Cardiovascular recovery — 60-second march with heart rate check
  5. Mobility — Overhead reach & seated forward bend

Frequently Asked Questions

What is functional biological age?

Functional biological age reflects how your body is actually aging based on physical performance markers — strength, balance, endurance, and mobility — rather than your birth date. Research shows these markers correlate with longevity and health outcomes.

How accurate is the assessment?

The assessment uses evidence-based tests validated in peer-reviewed research, including the 30-second chair stand test and single-leg balance test. Results are scored against age-adjusted benchmarks to provide an educational estimate of your functional age.

Is my data private?

Yes. All assessment data stays on your device and is not uploaded to any servers. You control whether to save or share your results.

Who should take this assessment?

The assessment is designed for healthy adults who want to understand their functional fitness level. If you have injuries, chronic conditions, or mobility limitations, consult a healthcare provider before attempting the physical tests.
