Entropy Age – Functional Biological Age Assessment

Entropy Age is a free 10-minute assessment that estimates your functional biological age through 5 at-home physical tests (no bloodwork needed). It measures strength, balance, endurance, and mobility to show if your body is aging faster or slower than your chronological age.

What We Measure

  • Lower-body strength — Chair sit-to-stand test
  • Muscular endurance — Wall sit hold
  • Balance — Single-leg balance test
  • Cardiovascular recovery — March test
  • Mobility — Overhead reach

FAQ

What is functional biological age?

Functional biological age reflects how your body is actually aging based on physical performance markers rather than your birth date.

Is the assessment accurate?

The assessment uses evidence-based tests validated in peer-reviewed research, including the 30-second chair stand test and single-leg balance test.

Is my data private?

Yes. All assessment data stays on your device and is not uploaded to any servers.
